Learner Opportunity Portal

BH&S Learners

Your learner profile is your opportunity file. The BH&S Learner Portal helps Batshas collect and update information about people who want to be considered for training opportunities.

Create your profile

Register once with your username, phone number, and secure password.

Keep details current

Update your contact details so BH&S can reach you when suitable opportunities become available.

Show your interest

Select the training areas you are interested in, such as learnerships, entrepreneurship, or digital literacy.

Wait for matching opportunities

Your information is kept by the BH&S Project Coordination Unit and used when programmes open.

How the portal works

  1. 01Register online and create your learner account with your phone number.
  2. 02Add accurate personal and contact details.
  3. 03Update your profile whenever your phone number or interests change.

Important: Keeping your profile updated improves the chances of being contacted when relevant training programmes become available.
